The
Challenge

Procurement processes that work - but consume far more time and coordination than they should.

For large housebuilders managing branded signage across hundreds of active sites, procurement is often more complex than it needs to be. Requirements arrive via spreadsheets, brand guidelines live in separate documents, and procurement teams spend hours reconciling requests.

  • Limited visibility of what is ordered across sites
  • Inconsistent brand application without visual references
  • Version control issues with shared spreadsheets
  • Manual administration for every order
  • Extended lead times from clarification cycles

The Solution

Working with Persimmon Homes, we built a branded digital procurement portal. Site managers browse approved signage with images, select variants, and submit orders directly. Procurement teams get structured data, full visibility, and a single source of truth.

One platform. Visual product selection. Structured ordering. Complete visibility from site to sign.

The Ordering Experience

Basket & Checkout

Selected items are summarised in a basket with images, quantities, and a full pricing breakdown including VAT.

At checkout, users select a contact, delivery site, and optional purchaser from saved records. A PO number and special instructions can be attached.

Previously saved contacts and sites make repeat ordering fast.

Structured Data

Every order captures:

  • Contact and site details
  • Product codes and quantities
  • PO reference
  • Special instructions

Clean, actionable data replaces ambiguous email requests.
